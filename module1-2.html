Lesson 1.2 – Cybersecurity in Daily Life

Cybersecurity affects everyone, every day. From online banking to social media, we constantly share personal information online. Practicing cybersecurity in daily life means being careful with what we click, what we share, and how we secure our devices.

  • Think before clicking suspicious links.
  • Log out of accounts when using shared computers.
  • Keep your software and apps updated.
