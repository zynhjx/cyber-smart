Lesson 1.3 – Identifying Online Threats

Online threats come in many forms, such as phishing, malware, and scams. Recognizing these threats early helps you avoid becoming a victim.

  • Phishing: Fake emails or messages pretending to be trustworthy sources.
  • Malware: Harmful software that damages devices or steals data.
  • Scams: Fake offers or requests to trick you into giving money or information.
