Lesson 1.1 – Introduction to Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, mobile devices, networks, and data from digital attacks. It is important because technology is part of our everyday lives— from online classes and communication to shopping and banking.

  • Protects sensitive information like passwords and personal data.
  • Prevents identity theft and online fraud.
  • Keeps individuals and organizations safe from hackers.
